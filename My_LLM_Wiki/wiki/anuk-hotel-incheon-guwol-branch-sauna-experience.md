## Anuk Hotel Guwol Branch Sauna Features
The [[Anuk Hotel Incheon Guwol Branch]] in [[Incheon]] is described as a hotel that is "serious about saunas," offering complimentary access for its guests.

Key features and observations:
*   **Interior Style:** The sauna boasts a classic/antique-style interior.
*   **Facilities:** It includes a [[dry sauna]] along with [[hot bath]] (온탕) and [[cold bath]] (냉탕) spaces.
*   **Temperatures:**
    *   [[Hot Bath]] (온탕): 42°C
    *   [[Cold Bath]] (냉탕): 19°C
    *   [[Sauna]] (사우나): 84°C
*   **Amenities:** Guests can enjoy watching TV from both the sauna and the cold bath.
*   **Privacy:** The visitor noted enjoying a private experience by visiting around 4 PM.
*   **Guest Access:** The sauna is free for hotel guests. A general adult price of 20,000 KRW is also mentioned, presumably for non-guests or as a value indicator.
