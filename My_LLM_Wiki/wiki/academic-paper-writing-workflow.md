This document outlines a recommended step-by-step workflow for writing academic papers, emphasizing a modular approach rather than attempting to complete the entire paper at once. It also provides an example of how [[AI agents]] can be utilized in the [[Draft Writing Mode]].

## Step-by-Step Paper Writing Modes

To effectively manage the complexity of academic paper writing, the process is divided into distinct modes:

1.  **[[Literature Search Mode]]**
    *   **Purpose:** Collection and organization of relevant literature.
    *   **Activities:** Identifying [[keywords]], searching for relevant papers, and systematically organizing the gathered resources.

2.  **[[Draft Writing Mode]]**
    *   **Purpose:** Structuring and drafting individual sections of the paper.
    *   **Activities:** Developing the framework for each section (e.g., Introduction, Methods, Results, Discussion) and generating initial content. This stage can leverage [[AI agents]] for content generation based on specific prompts.

3.  **[[Verification Mode]]**
    *   **Purpose:** Ensuring the internal consistency and accuracy of the draft.
    *   **Activities:** Checking the logical flow between arguments, verifying the accuracy and proper formatting of [[citations]] (e.g., [[APA Citation]]), and ensuring that all data presented is consistent and correctly interpreted.

4.  **[[Final Organization Mode]]**
    *   **Purpose:** Refining the language and presentation of the paper.
    *   **Activities:** Performing grammar checks, correcting stylistic issues, and ensuring adherence to specific formatting guidelines.

## Example Prompt for AI Agents in Draft Writing Mode

Here is an example of a [[prompt]] that can be used with an [[AI agent]] during the [[Draft Writing Mode]] to assist with generating content for specific sections:

```
[Draft Writing Mode]
Title: How to Effectively Utilize AI Agents
1. Introduction 200 words
2. Methods 300 words
3. Results 200 words
Insert at least one APA citation (2023-2025) in each section, and check the logical flow.
```

This prompt guides the AI agent to generate specific sections with word count targets and a requirement for contemporary [[APA Citation]] and [[Logical Flow]] review.
