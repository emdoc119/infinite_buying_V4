## JEPQ vs. K-JEPQ Discussion

User "jeok.lip.tu.ja" initiates a discussion comparing the popular US-listed ETF [[JEPQ]] (JPMorgan Equity Premium Income ETF) with its Korean equivalent, [[K-JEPQ]]. From a [[Long-Term Investment]] perspective, the user states they cannot find a compelling reason to choose K-JEPQ over JEPQ.

Key points of comparison and advantages cited for JEPQ (or arguments against K-JEPQ):

*   **Existing Domestic Covered Call ETFs**: The Korean market already offers similar [[Covered Call]] products, such as:
    *   [[TIGER US Nasdaq 100 Target Daily Covered Call]]
    *   [[RISE US Tech 100 Daily Fixed Covered Call]]
*   **Advantages of JEPQ**: JEPQ offers benefits like a strong [[주가성장률]] (stock growth rate), attractive [[배당률]] (dividend yield), and eligibility for [[연저펀ISA]] (Individual Savings Account and Pension Fund) benefits.
*   **Currency Exposure**: Investing in JEPQ provides direct [[Currency Exposure]] to the US dollar, which is considered an advantage by the user.

The user seeks further insights, asking if there's "anything I don't know" from experts.
